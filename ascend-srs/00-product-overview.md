## What Is Ascend?

Ascend is a **premium real-life RPG** where users level up themselves through quests, arcs, streaks, and progression systems.

Users don't just track habits. They:
- Build discipline
- Complete quests
- Level up stats
- Compete in leagues
- Unlock achievements
- Evolve their identity

Through **Arc Mode**.

---

## Core Game Systems

### 1. Player Stats System

Each user has RPG stats:

| Stat | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Strength | Fitness consistency |
| Wisdom | Reading/learning |
| Discipline | Habit completion |
| Focus | Deep work |
| Vitality | Sleep + hydration |
| Charisma | Social confidence |

Real-life actions increase stats:
- Gym → Strength +15 XP
- Reading → Wisdom +10 XP
- Deep Work → Focus +20 XP

---

### 2. Leveling System

Users gain XP daily with non-linear exponential progression:
- Level 1 → 100 XP
- Level 2 → 282 XP
- Level 5 → 1,118 XP
- Level 10 → 3,162 XP
- Level 50 → 35,355 XP
- Level 100 → 100,000+ XP

Formula: `XP_Required = 100 × Level^1.5`

---

### 3. Daily Quest Engine

Tasks become quests:
- **Main Quests** — High impact daily habits
- **Side Quests** — Optional bonus tasks
- **Daily Missions** — Recurring activities
- **Weekly Challenges** — Long-term goals
- **Epic Quests** — Major milestones

---

### 4. Streak Psychology Engine

Major retention system:
- Streak freezing
- Streak shields
- Comeback bonus
- Redemption mode
- Anti-burnout recovery
- Combo multipliers (7-day: ×1.2, 30-day: ×2.0)

---

### 5. Hard Mode

Premium differentiator with consequences for missed habits:
- XP deduction
- Stat decay
- League demotion
- Streak damage
- Penalty tasks
- Optional money stakes

---

### 6. League & Ranking System

Competitive tiers:
Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Master → Ascendant

Weekly competition across:
- Discipline
- Focus
- Fitness
- Consistency

---

### 7. Season System

Every 60–90 days:
- New challenges
- New themes
- New rewards
- Seasonal badges
- Exclusive cosmetics

---

### 8. Avatar / Identity System

Unlock:
- Avatars
- Themes
- Titles
- Aura effects
- Profile animations

---

### 9. Boss Battle System

Challenge-based progression:
- "Beat Procrastination Boss" — Complete 20 focus sessions in 7 days
- "Discipline Demon" — 30-day streak
- Multi-stage bosses with legendary rewards

---

### 10. AI Life Coach (Premium)

- Habit analysis
- Motivation detection
- Burnout prediction
- Schedule optimization
- Adaptive difficulty
