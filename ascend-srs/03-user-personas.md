# 03 — User Personas

# Ascend — Enter Arc Mode

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Age Group
18–35

### User Types
1. Self-Improvement Enthusiasts
2. Students
3. Working Professionals
4. Gym & Fitness Users
5. Gamers seeking progression systems

---

## Persona 1 — The Discipline Builder

### Profile
- **Name:** Aarav Sharma
- **Age:** 24
- **Occupation:** Software Developer
- **Income:** ₹8–15 LPA
- **Lifestyle:** Inconsistent but ambitious

### Goals
- Wake up early
- Reduce procrastination
- Become disciplined
- Gym consistency
- Improve focus

### Pain Points
- Inconsistent habits
- Dopamine addiction
- Doom scrolling
- Starts but quits

### Emotional Trigger
Feels guilty about wasted potential.

> "I know I can do more, but I'm inconsistent."

### Retention Drivers
- Streaks (strongest motivator)
- Leveling system
- Progress visualization
- Punishment mode (Hard Mode)

### Churn Risk
High if:
- Onboarding too long
- No quick wins
- Feels overwhelming

### Recommended Arc
- **Primary:** Monk Arc
- **Secondary:** Beast Mode Arc

### Monetization Potential
**Very High** — Likely premium features: AI coach, hard mode, accountability contracts

## Persona 3 — The Competitive Achiever

### Profile
- **Age:** 20–30
- **Psychology:** Extremely competitive
- **Motivation:** Winning, ranking, visibility

### Goals
- Top leaderboard positions
- Guild leadership
- Social recognition

### Emotional Trigger
> "I want to be top 10."

### Retention Drivers
- Weekly league competition
- Guild battles
- Visible rank
- Rewards for top performers

### Churn Risk
Low — Competition keeps them engaged as long as matchmaking is fair

### Recommended Arc
- **Primary:** Beast Mode Arc

### Monetization Potential
**High** — Will pay for competitive advantages (cosmetics, not P2W)

---

## Persona 4 — The Lost Beginner

### Profile
- **Age:** 18–28
- **Problem:** Doesn't know where to start
- **Confidence:** Low

### Goals
- Find direction
- Build basic routine
- Gain confidence

### Pain Points
- Overwhelmed by options
- Low confidence
- No existing routine

### Emotional Trigger
Needs direction.

> "Tell me what to do."

### Retention Drivers
- Guided onboarding
- Prebuilt arcs
- Daily quests (no decision fatigue)
- Small wins

### Churn Risk
**Very High** if:
- Too many choices
- No guidance
- Feels complex

### Recommended Arc
- **Primary:** Starter Arc
- **Secondary:** Monk Lite Arc

### Monetization Potential
**Low initially** — Convert to premium after engagement builds

---

## Persona 5 — The Fitness Warrior

### Profile
- **Age:** 20–32
- **Focus:** Body transformation
- **Motivation:** Physical results

### Goals
- Body transformation
- Workout consistency
- Nutrition discipline

### Retention Drivers
- Stat growth (Strength)
- Streaks
- Body progression tracking
- Competition

### Recommended Arc
- **Primary:** Warrior Arc

### Monetization Potential
**Medium-High** — Interested in advanced tracking and accountability

---

## Persona 6 — The Self-Improvement Addict

### Profile
- **Age:** 20–30
- **Behavior:** Consumes self-help content, productivity videos, motivation reels
- **Problem:** Consumes too much, executes too little

### Emotional Trigger
Transformation fantasy — wants to feel like they're improving without doing the hard work.

### Retention Drivers
- Identity system (titles, evolution)
- Prestige levels
- Boss battles
- Visual transformation

### Churn Risk
Medium — May engage superficially without deep habit change

### Recommended Arc
- **Primary:** Beast Mode Arc (forces execution)

### Monetization Potential
**Very High** — Attracted to premium identity features

## Onboarding Path by Persona

### Discipline Builder
Assessment → Monk Arc recommended → Hard Mode suggested → First quest: "Wake before 7 AM"

### Career Grinder
Assessment → Creator Arc recommended → Focus quests → First quest: "30-min coding session"

### Competitive Achiever
Assessment → Beast Mode Arc → League preview → First quest: "Complete 5 tasks today"

### Lost Beginner
Assessment → Starter Arc → Simplified UI → First quest: "Drink water" (easy win)

### Fitness Warrior
Assessment → Warrior Arc → Gym quests → First quest: "10-min workout"
